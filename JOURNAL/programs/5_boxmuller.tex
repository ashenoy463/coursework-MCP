\vspace{2em}

\header{Aim:} To sample two uniformly distributed random variables and calculate their Box-Muller transform, verifying that the result is normally distributed.\\


The Box-Muller transform of two uniformly distributed random variables $u$ and $v$ is given by

\begin{align*}
    x(u,v) &= \cos({2\pi v})\sqrt{-2 \ln u} \\
    y(u,v) &= \sin({2\pi v})\sqrt{-2 \ln u} 
\end{align*}
